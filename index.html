Mental Arithmetic Quiz

How quick can you solve the questions?

Rules
1. The game consists of level one three, with an increase level of difficulty.
2. The questions are mixture of addition, subtraction, multiplication and division.
3. There are 5 questions per level.
4. You have 30 seconds per question.
5. Enter your answer for each question.
6. You'll get points on every correct answer.
7. No calculator allowed.
8. Click next to move on to the next question.
